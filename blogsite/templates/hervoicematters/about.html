About Us

On daily basis we come across women facing different kinds of oppression and many agonizing difficulties that do not foster gender parity. All this ranges from health issues(during child birth), education (girls drop out due to pregnancies), women positioning at work places, cultural practices like FGM,and the most prevalent one, gender based violence including sexual assults.


This propelled the start of this initiative in Kenya and all over the world to create awareness and be a source of power to overcome these struggles.

Her voice matters initiative aims at empowering women by providing counseling, social support as well as instilling self esteem and hope by letting the world listen to HER and changing the world perception of her strength and abilities and giving her that chance. That shred of equality that she has ever desired. We will highlight numerous activities, stories and ideas that girls and women do and face thus encourage them to sharpen their talents through fashion, food, writing, poetry, beauty and any other skill that will empower her. You are welcome to join and get involved. Thank you
